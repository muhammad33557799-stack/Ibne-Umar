ABOUT US

Nurturing Hearts,
Building Scholars,
Serving the Ummah.

Jamia Abdullah Bin Umar (Lahore) is dedicated to providing comprehensive Islamic education rooted in the Quran and Sunnah, combined with academic discipline and spiritual growth. Our aim is to develop individuals who are knowledgeable, pious, and responsible members of society.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

To provide authentic Islamic education and tarbiyah that produces righteous scholars and responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of Islamic learning, spiritual development, and character building in Pakistan.

Our Tarbiyah

We focus on building strong imaan, noble character, and a lifelong commitment to Islamic values.

OUR EDUCATIONAL SYSTEM

A Balanced Approach to Islamic Education

Our structured system combines traditional Islamic scholarship with modern teaching methodologies to ensure depth of knowledge, clarity of understanding, and excellence in character.

Quranic Foundation

Strong foundation in Quran recitation, memorization, and understanding.

Islamic Sciences

Study of Hadith, Fiqh, Aqeedah, Tafsir, Arabic Grammar, and other classical texts.

Character Building

Focus on Akhlaq, discipline, tazkiyah, and community responsibility.

Practical Application

Encouraging students to implement knowledge to benefit daily life and society.

Continuous Growth

Lifelong learning mindset with regular revision and advanced studies.
