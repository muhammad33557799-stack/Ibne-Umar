ABOUT THE MAGAZINE

Thirty Years of Illuminating
Minds & Hearts

Ilm-o-Amal — meaning "Knowledge and Action" — is a monthly Islamic magazine published by Jamia Abdullah Bin Umar, Lahore. Since its founding, it has served as a trusted voice for authentic Islamic scholarship, delivering carefully researched articles to readers across Pakistan and beyond.

Grounded in the Quran and Sunnah, every issue carries the spirit of its name: bridging the gap between knowledge and righteous practice, and reminding believers that true knowledge (ilm) is inseparable from faithful practice (amal).

◆

Scholarly Rigor

◆

Complex theological topics presented in simple, relatable Urdu.

◆

Source Integrity

All narrations and rulings are verified with classical citations.

◆

WHAT'S INSIDE

Topics That Inspire & Educate

Each monthly issue is thoughtfully curated with a rich variety of Islamic content, ensuring something meaningful for every reader — student, scholar, or seeker.

COLUMN I

Quranic Insights

Tafsir, thematic Quranic studies, and reflections that deepen understanding of the Divine Word and its practical relevance in modern life.

COLUMN II

Hadith Studies

Explanations and commentary on authentic Ahadith, highlighting the Sunnah of the Prophet and the wisdom within his teachings.

COLUMN III

Islamic Jurisprudence

Scholarly Fiqh discussions covering contemporary issues with classical evidence, helping readers navigate modern life according to Islamic law.

COLUMN IV

Moral Guidance

Articles on Tazkiyah, character development, daily spiritual practice, and building a stronger relationship with Allah.

COLUMN V

Scholarly Articles

In-depth pieces authored by qualified Ulama on theology, history, Islamic civilisation, and contemporary Muslim affairs.

COLUMN VI

Family & Community

Practical guidance on family values, parenting in Islam, community responsibilities, and the role of the Muslim in society.

OUR LEGACY

A Trusted Voice for
Three Decades

Since its first issue, Ilm-o-Amal has remained committed to the highest standards of Islamic scholarship. Published under the supervision of Jamia Abdullah Bin Umar's learned faculty, every article undergoes careful review to ensure authenticity, accuracy, and relevance.

Distributed monthly across Lahore and throughout Pakistan, the magazine has built a loyal readership of students, teachers, professionals, and families who rely on it as a source of genuine Islamic nourishment.
