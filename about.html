OUR STORY

A Legacy of Knowledge & Faith

Jamia Abdullah Bin Umar was founded in 1994 with a singular vision: to provide the Muslim community of Lahore — and beyond — with an institution that blends the depth of traditional Islamic scholarship with the discipline and rigour of modern academic learning.

Named after the great Companion of the Prophet ﷺ, Abdullah ibn Umar (رضي الله عنه), known for his steadfast adherence to the Sunnah, the Jamia carries his legacy in its culture, curriculum, and character. Every student who walks through these doors is guided to not only acquire knowledge, but to embody it.

Over three decades, we have produced hundreds of graduates who serve as Huffaz, Ulama, teachers, and community leaders across Pakistan and internationally.
